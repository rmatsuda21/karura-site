Karura
Project

KARURA Project is an innovative student-driven initiative aimed at propelling international space exploration forward while crafting a cutting-edge Mars rover for the prestigious Mars Exploration Contest URC.

In a collaborative effort, 60 trailblazing students hailing from Japan and the U.S. will synergize their talents, surmounting linguistic, cultural, and time zone barriers. They'll harness a blend of online and offline methodologies to realize this audacious vision.

This endeavor is a convergence of diverse technologies and cultures, fostering inspiration and mutual growth among participants. Its impact extends beyond borders, nurturing a global network of skilled individuals primed for the future of space exploration.

With an unwavering passion for space exploration, we're dedicated to fashioning this project into an emblematic milestone in the realm of international student-led space development.
